Our Story

The Bump Family Legacy

Four generations of craftsmanship, reliability, and personalized service — built on a foundation of trust.

Family-Owned & Operated

Building Fences Since 1988

What started as a small family operation in Bridgewater, Massachusetts has grown into one of the South Shore's most trusted fence contractors. Four generations of the Bump family have poured their expertise into every fence we build.

We're not a faceless corporation — we're your neighbors. When you call Inline Fence, you're getting the personal attention and quality workmanship that only a family business can provide. We treat every property like it's our own.

From residential privacy fences to large-scale commercial installations, our commitment remains the same: honest assessments, quality materials, expert installation, and fences that stand the test of time.
